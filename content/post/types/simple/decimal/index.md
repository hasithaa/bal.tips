{{</md>}}
{{<md class="syntax">}}

{{</md>}}
{{<md class="tldr">}} 
* Floating Point Literal, e.g. `12.34`,
* Lang Library : [*ballerina/lang.decimal*]({{< linkh "#language-library---ballerinalangdecimal">}})
